Disclosure Document – Falcon Futures

This Disclosure Document is prepared in accordance with the SEBI (Research Analyst) Regulations, 2014. It aims to provide clients and prospective subscribers with essential information regarding the nature of our research and recommendation services. The goal is to support informed decision-making before availing of our services.

1. Research Entity Information

Falcon Futures is a private limited company, herein referred to as the “Research Entity” for the purposes of this document.

2. About the Research Entity

Registration: Officially registered under relevant regulatory guidelines as a Research Analyst since June 27, 2024.
Services Offered: We provide independent research and tailored investment recommendations designed to align with the financial goals and risk profiles of our clients.

3. Terms & Conditions

The terms and conditions governing our research and recommendation services are detailed in a separate document. Clients are encouraged to review those terms thoroughly before subscribing to our services.

4. Disciplinary History

There are no outstanding legal proceedings, material litigations, or regulatory actions against Falcon Futures, its analysts, associates, or their immediate relatives.

5. Associates

The Research Entity currently has no associated entities.

6. Disclosures Regarding Research & Recommendation Services

  • The Research Entity, its analysts, or their relatives may hold financial interests or beneficial ownership in the securities mentioned in our recommendations. These holdings, if any, are disclosed in our monthly reporting.
  • We maintain a strict policy of objectivity and independence. There are no conflicts of interest that could compromise the integrity of our research.
  • Our analysts or their associates may hold 1% or more of the equity securities of companies we cover; such holdings are disclosed monthly.
  • We do not have any affiliations or connections with the issuers of the securities we recommend.
  • No consideration, remuneration, or compensation has been received from any subject company for any form of research, investment banking, merchant banking, brokerage, or related services.
  • We have not accepted any non-investment banking compensation from subject companies in the last 12 months.
  • No benefits, either monetary or non-monetary, have been accepted in exchange for research publications or investment recommendations.
  • The subject companies mentioned in our research reports have not been clients of Falcon Futures during the preceding 12 months.
  • None of our analysts or associates hold positions as officers, directors, or employees in any subject company.
  • Falcon Futures does not participate in market-making activities for any of the securities mentioned in our research reports.

Conclusion

Falcon Futures is committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency, ethical conduct, and client-first research practices. This Disclosure Document is designed to foster trust and ensure that all stakeholders are aware of our approach to responsible financial research.
